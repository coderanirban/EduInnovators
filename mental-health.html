Integrated Mental Health and Well-being Tools

Daily Check-ins and Mood Tracking

Students begin their day with a quick emotional check-in, which tracks their mood and mental state. Based on the results, the platform can recommend activities, such as mindfulness exercises, relaxation games, or even suggest taking a break.

Mindful Challenges and Rewards

Incorporate well-being challenges, such as “Take 10 minutes for mindfulness,” “Complete a gratitude journal entry,” or “Do a 5-minute stretch.” Completing these challenges earns students well-being badges and boosts their in-game score.

Virtual Counseling and Support

The platform could offer virtual counseling sessions, either through AI-driven chatbots that provide immediate support or by connecting students with real counselors. Additionally, there could be resources like articles, videos, or podcasts focused on mental health topics.

Peer Support Communities

Create spaces where students can support each other. These could be moderated forums or chat groups where students share experiences, challenges, and encouragement. Gamification can also be applied here, with rewards for positive engagement and support within the community.
